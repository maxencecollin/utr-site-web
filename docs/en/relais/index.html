Share the adventure as a relay! Experience the Tour de la Ria as a duo, between Locoal-Mendon and Sainte-Hélène

  • Read the rules
  • Entry requirements

The first runner links Locoal-Mendon to Sainte-Hélène, crossing the Ria d'Étel by boat, before handing over to their partner, who completes the loop back to Locoal-Mendon.
